Our Story

How it began

  1. April 2026

    The first hello

    A mutual friend's rooftop dinner, one shared plate of pani puri, and a conversation that went on far too long. Neither of us wanted to leave first.

  2. May 2026

    The proposal

    On a monsoon evening in Raipur, with the rain just easing, Alok asked and Pratibha said yes before he finished the sentence.

    The wedding

    Two families, four days of celebration, and one very long guest list. We can't wait to have you with us.
